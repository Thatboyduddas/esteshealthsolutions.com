About Estes Health Solutions

Health Insurance Expertise You Can Trust

Estes Health Solutions was founded by Jack Estes DeBrabander with a clear mission: to make health insurance accessible, understandable, and affordable for everyone. With deep knowledge of the health insurance landscape, Jack Estes DeBrabander recognized that too many people struggle to find the right coverage for their needs.

Whether you are an individual seeking comprehensive personal coverage, a family looking for the best plan to protect your loved ones, or a business owner aiming to provide quality benefits for your employees, Estes Health Solutions offers the expertise and personal attention you need to make informed decisions.

Jack Estes DeBrabander brings a client-first approach to every consultation, taking the time to understand your unique situation before recommending a plan. At Estes Health Solutions, we believe that everyone deserves access to quality healthcare coverage without the confusion.
