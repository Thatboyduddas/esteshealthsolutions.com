Health Insurance Services

Comprehensive health insurance consulting services from Estes Health Solutions, led by Jack Estes DeBrabander.

Estes Health Solutions offers a full spectrum of health insurance consulting services designed to meet the needs of individuals, families, and organizations. Jack Estes DeBrabander works closely with each client to understand their specific situation and deliver tailored recommendations that balance quality coverage with affordability. Below you will find detailed information about each of our core service offerings.

1

Individual & Family Health Insurance

Finding the right health insurance plan for yourself or your family is one of the most important financial decisions you can make. The wrong plan can mean paying thousands more than necessary in premiums, or discovering gaps in coverage when you need care the most. At Estes Health Solutions, Jack Estes DeBrabander takes the guesswork out of health insurance shopping by conducting a thorough analysis of your needs and matching you with plans that provide the best value.

The individual and family health insurance market offers a wide range of options, from high-deductible health plans paired with Health Savings Accounts to comprehensive PPO and HMO plans with lower out-of-pocket costs. Jack DeBrabander evaluates each option based on your medical history, preferred healthcare providers, prescription needs, and financial situation. We compare plans across multiple carriers to ensure you see the full picture before making a decision.

Estes Health Solutions also assists clients with understanding and applying for premium subsidies through the Affordable Care Act marketplace. Many individuals and families qualify for significant financial assistance but are unaware of their eligibility or find the application process confusing. Jack Estes DeBrabander guides you through every step, from determining subsidy eligibility to completing enrollment, making sure you receive every dollar of assistance you are entitled to.

  • Comprehensive plan comparison across multiple insurance carriers
  • ACA marketplace enrollment and subsidy eligibility assessment
  • Health Savings Account (HSA) and Flexible Spending Account (FSA) guidance
  • Short-term and catastrophic plan options for transitional coverage
  • Annual plan review and open enrollment assistance
2

Group Health Insurance

For employers, offering quality health insurance is essential to attracting and retaining talented employees. However, designing a group health plan that meets the needs of a diverse workforce while staying within budget can be a significant challenge. Jack Estes DeBrabander and Estes Health Solutions specialize in helping businesses of all sizes build competitive benefits packages that employees value and employers can sustain.

Our group health insurance consulting begins with a detailed assessment of your organization's workforce demographics, budget parameters, and strategic goals. We then survey the market, soliciting proposals from multiple insurance carriers and evaluating each one based on plan design, network adequacy, cost structure, and administrative requirements. Jack Estes DeBrabander presents a clear comparison of the top options, including projected costs for both the employer and employees, so you can make an informed decision.

Beyond initial plan selection, Estes Health Solutions provides ongoing support for group health plan administration. This includes assistance with employee enrollment, benefits communication, compliance with federal and state regulations such as the Employer Shared Responsibility Provision under the ACA, and annual plan renewals. Jack DeBrabander works as a true partner to your business, helping you navigate the complexities of group health insurance so you can focus on running your organization.

  • Small group and large group health plan design and placement
  • Multi-carrier proposal analysis and negotiation
  • Employee benefits communication and enrollment support
  • ACA compliance guidance and reporting assistance
  • Annual renewal management and cost containment strategies
  • Self-funded and level-funded plan evaluation
3

Medicare & Supplemental Insurance

Turning 65 or becoming eligible for Medicare is a major milestone, but it also comes with a host of decisions that can feel overwhelming. Original Medicare, Medicare Advantage, Medigap policies, Part D prescription drug plans, and various supplemental options all have different benefits, costs, and enrollment rules. Jack Estes DeBrabander helps seniors and their families navigate this complex landscape with confidence.

At Estes Health Solutions, our Medicare consulting service begins with a comprehensive review of your current health needs, medications, and preferred healthcare providers. Jack Estes DeBrabander then explains each Medicare option in clear, straightforward terms, highlighting the differences between Original Medicare with a supplement versus Medicare Advantage plans. We analyze the specific plans available in your area, comparing monthly premiums, annual out-of-pocket limits, drug formularies, and provider networks.

Enrollment timing is critical with Medicare, as missing key deadlines can result in penalties and coverage gaps. Jack DeBrabander ensures that clients understand their Initial Enrollment Period, Special Enrollment Periods, and Annual Enrollment Period options. Estes Health Solutions also assists clients who need to transition from employer-sponsored coverage to Medicare, coordinating the timing to avoid any lapse in protection. Whether you are newly eligible or looking to review your current Medicare plan, Jack Estes DeBrabander provides the expert guidance you need.

  • Original Medicare (Part A and Part B) enrollment guidance
  • Medicare Advantage (Part C) plan comparison and selection
  • Medigap (Medicare Supplement) policy analysis
  • Part D prescription drug plan evaluation
  • Employer-to-Medicare transition planning
  • Annual Medicare plan review during Open Enrollment
4

Health Insurance Consulting & Strategy

Beyond plan selection and enrollment, many individuals and organizations benefit from strategic health insurance consulting. Whether you are a business looking to optimize your benefits spend, an individual trying to understand a complex medical billing situation, or a family planning for future healthcare needs, Estes Health Solutions provides the analytical expertise and industry knowledge to help you make smarter decisions.

Jack Estes DeBrabander offers health insurance audits that evaluate your current coverage against your actual needs and usage patterns. These audits often reveal opportunities for significant savings, whether through plan redesign, carrier switching, or taking advantage of tax-advantaged accounts. For businesses, Jack DeBrabander provides benchmarking analysis that compares your benefits offering against industry standards, helping you understand where you stand competitively and where adjustments could improve employee satisfaction or reduce costs.

Estes Health Solutions also assists with claims advocacy and dispute resolution. Navigating a denied claim or an unexpected medical bill can be stressful and time-consuming. Jack Estes DeBrabander works on behalf of clients to communicate with insurance carriers, review claims decisions, and pursue appeals when appropriate. Our goal is to ensure that you receive the full benefits your plan provides and that billing errors or coverage misunderstandings are resolved quickly and fairly.

  • Health insurance coverage audits and optimization
  • Benefits benchmarking and competitive analysis
  • Claims advocacy and dispute resolution
  • Regulatory compliance consulting
  • Cost containment strategy development
  • Health insurance literacy education and employee training
